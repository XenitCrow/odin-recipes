Burguer Recipe

Description

HThere’s something timeless about a good burger—the way the heat kisses the meat, the crisp of fresh lettuce, and the satisfying crunch as you bite into toasted bread. This simple burger doesn’t aim for complexity, but instead captures the essence of comfort food done right.

Whether you're looking for a quick dinner or a base to build your own gourmet creation, this recipe delivers the fundamentals with balance and flavor. Add your own sauces or toppings to make it yours—but even in its simplest form, it hits the spot every time.

Ingredients

  • 2 breads
  • A meat
  • Tomato
  • Onion
  • Lettuce
  • Some ajonjoli

Steps

  1. Toast both pieces of bread until golden
  2. Cook the meat to your preferred doneness
  3. Place one bread slice on a plate
  4. Add lettuce, then the cooked meat
  5. Slice tomato and onion; layer them on top
  6. Cover whit the second bread slice
  7. Sprinkle ajonjoli on top for flavor and style
  8. Enjoy!
