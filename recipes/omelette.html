Omelette Recipe

Descripion

The humble omelette—quick to make, easy to love. This recipe invites you to start your day with something warm, soft, and filled with flavor. With just a few simple ingredients, it transforms your breakfast into a moment of quiet delight.

Softly sautéed vegetables melt into a bed of eggs, and a touch of cheese ties it all together. Perfect for slow mornings, light lunches, or even a gentle dinner, this omelette reminds us that simple food, done well, can be deeply satisfying.

Ingredientes

  • 2 eggs
  • A pinch of salt
  • Diced onion
  • Bell pepper
  • Cheese
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Crack the eggs into a bowl and whisk with salt
  2. Melt butter in a pan over medium heat
  3. Add diced onion and bell pepper, cook briefly
  4. Pour in the eggs and let them set
  5. Sprinkle cheese on top
  6. Fold the omelette and serve warm
