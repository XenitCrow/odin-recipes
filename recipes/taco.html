Taco recipe

Description

A taco doesn’t ask for much—just a warm tortilla, flavorful fillings, and a moment of your time. This quick taco recipe brings together tender chicken, crisp lettuce, and creamy toppings for a burst of flavor in every bite.

It’s fast, fun, and endlessly flexible. Perfect for when you’re short on time but hungry for something bold. Whether you’re making a single serving or prepping a plateful for guests, these tacos are sure to satisfy without stress.

Ingredients

  • 1 tortilla
  • Cooked chicken
  • Shredded cheese
  • Lettuce
  • Salsa
  • Sour cream

Steps

  1. Warm the tortilla on a pan or skillet
  2. Place cooked chicken in the center
  3. Add shredded cheese over the chicken
  4. Top with lettuce and a spoon of salsa
  5. Add a small dollop of sour cream
  6. Fold the tortilla and serve
